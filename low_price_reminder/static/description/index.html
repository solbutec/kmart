Low Sale Price Reminder

This module sends reminder email with list of product which have sale price lower then cost price!

Company Configuration

Configure products to track prices and also configure users who will get notification email!

  • Product Filter = All: Consider all products of system.
  • Product Filter = Product Category: Consider products belongs from selected categories.
  • Product Filter = All: Consider selected products.
  • Selected Users will get notification email with pdf.

Scheduler

Scheduler runs every day and send email with products!


Email Template


Sent Email to configured users on company form


Attachment of pdf report in email


Generated pdf with list of products with sale and cost price
